How compliance issues move through the framework

Issue Identification
A compliance issue is identified through various channels: regulatory findings, internal audits, self-identification, or ongoing monitoring. The issue enters the CIMO framework for proper oversight.

Based on the issue characteristics, it's assigned to the appropriate oversight tier. This determines the level of CIMO involvement and control requirements.
