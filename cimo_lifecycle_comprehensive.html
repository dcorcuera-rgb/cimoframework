  • Tier 1 (High-Rated Regulatory): Gets enhanced oversight at every critical step - from comprehensive documentation to board-level visibility and strict change controls
  • Tier 2 (Extensions Pattern): Standard processes with conditional enhancements triggered by extension pattern - pre-validation and enhanced change management applied
  • Tier 2 (Customer + Engineering): Standard processes with targeted enhancements for customer impact and engineering coordination - no unnecessary overhead
  • Tier 3 (Standard Moderate): Essential IM program requirements only - efficient processing without over-engineering
  • Universal Elements: All examples receive core requirements (SLA tracking, in-line QA, governance access, standard validation)
  • Smart Differentiation: Framework applies additional oversight precisely where risk characteristics warrant it, avoiding bureaucracy for standard issues
  • Conditional Logic: Tier 2 examples show how specific risk factors (extensions, customer impact, engineering dependencies) trigger targeted enhancements
